Georgians Academy for Professional Studies (GAPS) was established in the year 2013 under the aegis of Georgians Educational Society(Non-Profit Organisation) with a passion of motivating and guiding the candidate to realize their dream of becoming an officer in the Armed Forces. GAPS is a dream project of Like minded people who are alumni of "King George Royal Indian Military College"(KGRIMC) presently known as Rashtriya Military School and Ex Armed forces officers and Educationists. We trained the Youngsters to join defence by providing well disciplined SSB Guidance program. The sole aim of establishing this institution is to provide a Platform to the students of Rashtriya Military Schools, Sainik schools, KendriyaÂ  Vidyalaya/Novadaya Vidyalaya and Wards of Ex-army personal where they can cherish their dream of wearing Uniform and serve the country.

We As a Team take the responsibility of analysing every individual candidate personality thoroughly and make them understand their strong and weak areas. Accordingly, prepare them for SSB Coaching / Written. We love to take the challenge of analysing the repeaters based on their previous SSB and help them to know their grey areas and achieve their aim by various mock tests.

We have dedicated team of faculty members having expertise in specific subjects and have developed high quality study material based on the continuous R & D and regular subject practice papers to analyse the preparation for every candidate.

Once we get hold of the candidate we never leave them until they achieve their aim by continuously providing them counselling and motivation according to their area of interest other than armed forces.

We Have been giving one of the best results in SSB preparations and NDA/CDS/AFCAT written Preparation. In a short span of 5 years we have been able to considered not only as best SSB institute in Delhi/NCR but across across whole country.

In order to encourage the student of economically weaker section, we provide our services free of cost.
