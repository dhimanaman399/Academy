Combined defence services (CDS II) 2019 was conducted on 8 September 2019 in three shift
a) English b) general studies c) Mathematics

These exams are conducted by UPSC every year twice for entry into the services of armed forces namely Army, Navy and Air force. All the graduate male/female candidates can apply according to the specific educational qualification criteria mentioned in the notification given by UPSC. After clearing the written exam the selected candidates are called for SSB at various SSB centres of Army, Navy and air force given as per the choice of the candidates.
After SSB recommended candidates go through the medical examinations and those who are medically fit gets the call to join respective academy.

a) Date of Exam 08/09/2019
b) Tentative date of result mid November
c) SSB January onwards ( for OTA April onward)
